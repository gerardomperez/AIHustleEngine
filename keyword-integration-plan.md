## FAQ CONTENT SUGGESTIONS (For Future Addition)

If adding an FAQ section, these Q&As target long-tail keywords:

**Q: What exactly is an AI side hustle?**  
*Targets: "AI side hustle"*  
A: An AI side hustle is a business you build using AI tools to automate work, reduce costs, and scale faster than traditional methods. Our AI business course teaches you proven AI income streams that require no coding.

**Q: Can I really make money with AI without technical skills?**  
*Targets: "make money with AI", "no-code AI business"*  
A: Absolutely. Most of our successful students started as complete beginners. The course focuses on no-code AI business strategies using tools like ChatGPT, Midjourney, and automation platforms.

**Q: How quickly can I start generating AI income streams?**  
*Targets: "AI income streams"*  
A: Many students land their first client or make their first sale within 2-4 weeks of starting the AI business course. The Accelerator tier includes done-for-you templates that speed this up significantly.

**Q: What's the difference between an AI automation business and a regular online business?**  
*Targets: "AI automation business"*  
A: An AI automation business uses AI tools to handle 80% of repetitive work — content creation, client communication, research, and delivery. This creates passive income with AI that scales without your constant involvement.

**Q: Do I need to know prompt engineering to succeed?**  
*Targets: "prompt engineering course"*  
A: Not before you start. Our prompt engineering course modules teach you everything from basics to advanced techniques. By Module 4, you'll write prompts that get better results than 95% of AI users.

**Q: What types of AI businesses does the course cover?**  
*Targets: "AI business ideas 2024/2026", "ChatGPT business", "AI content creation business", "AI prompt business"*  
A: We cover AI content creation businesses, AI prompt businesses, ChatGPT business models, AI freelance business structures, and how to automate existing businesses with AI. You choose the path that fits your goals.
