How to Start an AI Copywriting Service (From Zero to First Client in 14 Days)

AI Hustle Engine • March 2026
If you had to pick one AI income model to start with, AI copywriting is hard to argue against. The demand is enormous — every business that has a website, runs ads, or sends emails needs copy, and most of them can't afford a traditional agency. The barriers to entry are low — you need ChatGPT, a text editor, and the ability to communicate clearly. And the economics are exceptional — with AI assistance, what used to take a copywriter 8 hours takes 2, without sacrificing quality. The challenge isn't the service — it's finding clients, pricing correctly, and building the workflow to deliver consistently. This guide covers all three. By the end, you'll have a clear service offering, a starting price point, and a week-by-week plan to get your first paying client. The goal for Week 1 is simple: one paid project. Not $10,000 — one project.
