🗺️ AI Business Blueprint

Your step-by-step roadmap from zero to $5K–$20K/month with AI. Follow each phase in order — don't skip ahead.

5 Phases 90-Day Roadmap $0 → $5K/mo Proven Framework
1

Foundation — Build Your Core Stack

⏱ Week 1–2 | Days 1–14
🎯 Goal: Have your tools, offer, and basic positioning ready to go to market
1

Set up your AI tool stack

Claude or GPT-4 for writing/reasoning, Perplexity for research, Make.com or Zapier for automation. Budget: free tiers or ~$40/mo max.

2

Define your one offer

Use the Income Stream Scoring Matrix to pick ONE income model. No pivoting in Phase 1. Write a one-paragraph offer description.

3

Build your minimum presence

LinkedIn profile updated with AI angle + one platform where clients can find you (Upwork, Fiverr, or personal site). Takes 2–3 hours max.

4

Create one portfolio sample

Generate a spec piece using AI that demonstrates your service quality. This is your proof-of-concept, not your masterpiece.

5

Validate before building

Complete the Service Validation Worksheet. Get at least 3 responses from target customers before writing another line of copy.

Phase 1 Milestone

✅ Tool stack live · ✅ One offer defined · ✅ Profile set up · ✅ First outreach sent

2

First Revenue — Land Your First Client

⏱ Week 2–4 | Days 7–30
🎯 Goal: Close your first paying client (even at a discount) to prove the model works
1

Send 10 personalized outreach messages daily

Use the Cold Email Sequence prompt from the vault. Personalize the first 2 sentences for every message. No templates sent cold.

2

Offer a pilot at 50% discount

Your first client gets a deal. You get a case study, testimonial, and real data. Frame it as "founding member" pricing — not desperation.

3

Nail the discovery call

Ask 5 questions, shut up, take notes. Goal: understand their actual problem, not pitch your service. Follow up with the Discovery Call Follow-Up template.

4

Deliver remarkable work

Overdeliver on your first project. Every future referral and case study depends on this client's experience.

5

Request a testimonial immediately

Ask within 48 hours of delivery while results are fresh. Use the Testimonial Request prompt from the vault.

Phase 2 Milestone

✅ First paid client · ✅ First testimonial · ✅ First case study documented

3

Momentum — Build to $2K/Month

⏱ Week 4–8 | Days 21–60
🎯 Goal: 2–4 paying clients, standardized workflow, consistent outreach
1

Raise your prices to full rate

After your first client, charge full price. Use the discount as proof, not as your permanent positioning.

2

Document your delivery process

Write a simple SOP for how you deliver your service. This makes you faster, consistent, and ready to delegate later.

3

Build a referral engine

Send the Referral Request email to every satisfied client. One referral can replace weeks of cold outreach.

4

Add one content channel

Pick one: LinkedIn posts, YouTube shorts, or Twitter/X threads. Post 3x/week. This is your inbound pipeline — it compounds over time.

5

Create a simple productized offering

Package your service into a defined deliverable with a fixed price and timeline. Eliminates custom scope creep.

Phase 3 Milestone

✅ $1K–$2K/mo revenue · ✅ Repeatable delivery process · ✅ Content channel active

4

Scale — $2K to $5K/Month

⏱ Week 8–12 | Days 45–90
🎯 Goal: Systematize delivery, add revenue streams, reduce time-per-dollar
1

Automate your delivery workflow

Use Make.com, Zapier, or n8n to automate repetitive parts of your service. Target: 30% time savings on each project.

2

Add a second revenue stream

If you're freelancing: add a digital product (template pack, mini-course). If you have a product: add a service tier. Diversify without splitting focus.

3

Raise prices again

At $2K+/mo, you have proof. Raise rates by 25–50%. Some clients will leave — that's fine. New clients pay the new rate.

4

Build your first email list

Create a lead magnet (checklist, template, mini-guide) and collect emails. Your email list is the only audience you own.

5

Consider your first hire or subcontractor

When you're turning away work, it's time to delegate. Start with a VA for admin, or a subcontractor for overflow delivery work.

Phase 4 Milestone

✅ $3K–$5K/mo revenue · ✅ Automated delivery · ✅ 200+ email subscribers

5

Acceleration — $5K to $20K/Month

⏱ Month 4–12
🎯 Goal: Build leverage — revenue not tied 1:1 to your time
1

Launch a premium offer ($2K–$10K)

High-ticket consulting, done-with-you programs, or agency retainers. One client at this level = 2–5 lower-tier clients.

2

Build or buy distribution

Paid ads, affiliate partnerships, or acquiring an audience. Organic content alone is too slow at this stage.

3

Create recurring revenue

Retainers, memberships, or SaaS. Recurring revenue makes your business predictable and sellable.

4

Document everything for hiring

Every process you do manually should be documented and delegatable. You're building a machine, not a job.

5

Protect and systematize

Legal (contracts, IP), financial (separate business accounts, tax planning), and operational (project management, CRM) systems.

Phase 5 Milestone

✅ $10K+/mo · ✅ Recurring revenue stream · ✅ Team or contractors supporting delivery
