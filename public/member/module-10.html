Using AI for financial decisions

This lesson covers how to identify ideal partnership candidates, structure win-win agreements, activate partners effectively, and build a partnership program that generates consistent referrals without constant maintenance. One strategic partnership I formed three years ago has sent me 23 clients. I've sent my partner 11 clients. That's combined $180,000+ in referred revenue from one relationship that takes 10 minutes a month to maintain. That's the power of the right partnership.

Key Takeaways

  • Partnership identification: Finding partners whose audience is your audience
